Fufu and Egusi Soup is a beloved dish in the Bangola Kingdom, showcasing the rich culinary traditions of West Africa. Fufu is a starchy side dish made from boiled and pounded cassava, yams, or plantains, while Egusi Soup is a flavorful stew made with ground melon seeds, vegetables, and meat or fish.
